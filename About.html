About Our Team

Our Story

Since 1975, Rucker's Restaurant has been a beloved staple in the Scottsdale community. Our founder, John Rucker, started his culinary journey as a farmer just up the road from where we sit today. We set out to create an upscale dining experience that combines exceptional cuisine with a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our Philosophy

A high quality meal starts with high quality ingriedients. We use only the freshest ingriedients tailored into each meal by talented chefs. We believe in farm to table foods to provide the best tasting ingriedients possible. .

Our Team

Our staff is not only like family, but wants you to feel the same as soon as you walk through our doors. From start to finish youll be greeted with a smile and treated to 5-star services for your entire dining expirience with us.

Our Commitment

At Rucker's, we're dedicated to sustainability and giving back to our community. We source our ingredients from the local farms and all of our meats and fish are prepared locally by our artisan butchers.
